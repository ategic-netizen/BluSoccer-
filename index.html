Bio & Timeline

Who is Theodore Blu?

I'm an Ivy League graduate, a world traveler, and a father. Before writing full-time about football, I served as a college professor—teaching critical thinking, communication, and the kind of pattern recognition that shows up on the pitch.

I believe the game is a classroom: tactics teach structure, culture teaches empathy, and competition teaches resilience.

Timeline

Today — Writing, coaching locally, and traveling for matches.
2010s — College professor; launched a community soccer literacy project.
2000s — Global travel across 4 continents; matches in London, Accra, Madrid, and Seattle.
Earlier — Ivy League degree; lifelong love affair with the ball.
